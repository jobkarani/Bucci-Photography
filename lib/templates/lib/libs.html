PHOTOGRAPHY IS MY PASSION

The focus of my photographic passion is nature photography. An exciting field in which I deal with macro photography as well as animal and landscape photography. On my site I would like to give you an insight into this interesting subject area, but also my view of things.

On long winter evenings or on days when, due to the weather, there is no fascinating motif to be found, I sometimes photograph people in my studio. But photographing water drops is particularly fascinating for me. An exciting topic, because what the eye cannot see, the camera catches. Be amazed when you see the water from a completely different perspective.
